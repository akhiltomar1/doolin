Lets Look at a few of our projects-

Seeing the steady increase in mental health problems, Aglet a group of psychological mental health researchers build an online portal to connect directly to individuals to the nearest and professional psysiatrists and doctors. The goal is to increase awareness about mental health problems and make it a common talk in society, especially for the youngsters. The logo is a simple rectangle with the letter A in negative.
Surely Nut is a UK based Peanut Butter brand with a taste like no other. The business was formed by 4 friends with their love for Peanut Butter. They've been recognised for their unique flavours combined with pean#letsdoolin Surely Nut strongly believes in sharing and therefore their tagline, 'Made to share' is about sharing their products and enjoying their unique peanut butter together. The brand concept was to create a brand that is bold and energetic, to be unique from the market and not use any peanut references in the identity and packaging design
Gurham is a group of partners who think architecture is more than just design and materials but it has the power to bring people closer while also helping nature. The objective of the project was to design a logo for the brand that is unique and helps it differentiate in the market while expressing the brand attributes. So we just did that.
